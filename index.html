Pete Carroll


Credit: http://bleacherreport.net/
  • Pete Carroll was the defensive coordinator for the San Fransisco 49ers from 1995-1996.
  • He then was the head coach of the New England Patriots from 1997-1999.
  • After that he moved to the head coach of USC from 2001-2009.
  • Currently he is the Seahawks headcoach, starting in 2010.
